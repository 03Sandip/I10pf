About Gonotes

Notes marketplace for students — curated, searchable, and preview-first.

Gonotes is built for students who want high-quality, peer-created study notes and summaries. Our mission is to make learning efficient by helping students find trustworthy notes, preview them, and buy only what they need. We value quality, transparency, and a friendly student-first experience.

What you'll find on Gonotes

  • Curated notes for college courses and entrance exams.
  • Preview samples so you can check quality before buying.
  • Search and filter by subject, semester, and stream.

Who we are

A small team of students and engineers focused on building tools for learning. Want to help or collaborate? Email us at hello@gonotes.shop.
